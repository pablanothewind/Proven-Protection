Services

Residential

Home is where you want to feel the safest. Protecting your family is your most important desire. Having a home alarm security and surveillance system installed can enhance your family's safety and provide you with peace of mind.

Commercial

Proven Protection is an experienced, reputable company that offers security services in the surrounding East TN area. We have a variety of available solutions in order to accommodate both the size of your building and your personal objectives.

Fire

Help protect your home in the event of a fire with our Fire Monitoring service. With this protection you will get the added benefit of alerting the Fire Department directly from your panel. This faster response time will give you peace of mind in an emergency.

Burglary

Home invasions happen every day and often thankfully when your family is out of the house. With Proven Protection, you know your family and home are monitored 24/7 so you know the minute it happens, not after. Should your family at any time be in danger, our monitoring service will respond fast and get help immediately.
